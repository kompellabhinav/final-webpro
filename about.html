Who We Are

I am Abhinav Kompella, a fitness enthusiasts who is passionate about helping people achieve their fitness goals. My mission is to provide a comprehensive workout tracking platform that empowers users to monitor their progress and stay motivated on their fitness journey.

Project Description

I chose to create the Workout Tracker website because I noticed a common challenge among fitness enthusiasts - keeping track of their workout progress. Many people struggle to remember their exercises, sets, and weights from previous workouts. I wanted to solve this problem by developing an easy-to-use platform where users can log and review their workouts conveniently.

My goal is to inspire individuals to stay committed to their fitness routines and celebrate their achievements along the way. I believe that consistent tracking of workouts leads to better performance and long-term fitness success.

Technology Used

For the Workout Tracker website, I utilized a combination of front-end and back-end technologies. The front-end is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, providing a responsive and visually appealing user interface. We also incorporated the Bootstrap framework for a consistent layout and responsive design across different devices.

On the back-end, we used node.js to handle user authentication, data storage, and interaction with the database. To store and manage workout data, we employed MongoDB as the database management system. I also used EJS to update the html automatically depending on size of data.
